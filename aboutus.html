We believe small steps lead to giant leaps for our planet.

EcoTrack was born from a simple idea: to empower every person in India with the knowledge to make a positive environmental impact.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make sustainability accessible and actionable for everyone. We aim to demystify the concept of a carbon footprint by providing a simple, localized, and intuitive tool. We want to transform climate anxiety into positive action, showing that individual efforts, when combined, create a powerful force for change.

Why We Are Doing This

For Our Shared Home

India's unique and beautiful ecosystems are facing unprecedented challenges. We are driven by a deep-seated desire to protect our natural heritage for future generations. We believe technology can be a powerful ally in this cause.

To Empower Individuals

The climate conversation can often feel overwhelming. We want to break it down to a personal level. By showing you the direct impact of your lifestyle, we empower you to become a conscious consumer and an agent of change in your community.

To Build a Community

EcoTrack is more than just a calculator; it's the start of a movement. We envision a future where millions of Indians are actively tracking their footprint and working together towards a common goal: a sustainable and thriving India.
